Road Bike Toyota

Vintage Toyota form 1975.

1975, 30000km, 125hp, black/metalic, gas, 4speed manual

What makes this bike so unique is its old-school design, which draws directly from vintage Toyota Land Cruisers. For example, the new Mosaic GT2-X features minimally-design interior metals, robust axels, and Land Cruiser-inspired colors, which include Desert Tane, Nebula Green, Dune Beige, and more. As a result, the bike evokes the adventure-seeking aura of the original Land Cruiser, compacting it down into a tough and eye-catching two-wheeler. Aside from aesthetics, the bike boasts some notable hardware and specs. For example, the premium bike features extra-wide titanium flat bars, a hand-crafted steel fork and titanium Mosica seat post, Shimano Deore XT disc brakes, and Industry Nine Trail 270 wheels.
